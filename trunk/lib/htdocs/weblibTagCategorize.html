Tags

Categories


 

 

Build a category of tags by editing them in the space in the center.

Put each tag on a separate line. Insert spaces in front of a tag to make it a subcategory. Insert more spaces for second or deeper levels of subcategories. A tag maybe used as many times as needed. Blank lines maybe used for clarity.

For example:

food
  fish
  fruit
    apple
    orange
  meat

travel
  bus
  train
  airplane

some tag (2) - some web page title,...
