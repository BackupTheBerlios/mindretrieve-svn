  Tag Name Categorize

Categorize the tags by editing them in the space in the right.

Put each tag on a line. Insert 2 spaces in front of a tag to mean it is a subcategory. A tag may appears more than one time (however some logical restriction, like no loops, still applies.)

For example:

food
  fish
  fruit
    apple
    orange
  meat
travel
  bus
  train
  airplane
