Getting Started

Welcome to MindRetrieve, the desktop tool to search and organize your personal web! Here are a few steps you can do to prepare your browser for MindRetrieve.

  1. Bookmark your personal MindRetrieve URL. Better yet, set it as your home page!
  2. Import your bookmarks. MindRetrieve help you search and organize bookmarked pages more effectively with the flexible tagging system.
  3. When your find an interesting webpage, use the MindRetrieve's bookmarklet to quickly add it to your web library. To add the bookmarklet to your browser, drag the link MindRetrieve to your browser's personal bar.
  4. Let MindRetrieve archive your browsing history for later search and retrieval . To enable archiving, simply configure your proxy to localhost:8051. Or follow the detail instructions.
